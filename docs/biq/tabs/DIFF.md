## Quint Layout Contract
Quint uses a difficulty list on the left and grouped numeric settings on the right.

### Panels
- `Universal Traits`
  - Numeric fields for:
    - `Citizens Born Content`
    - `Citizens Quelled Per Military Unit`
    - `Attack Bonus vs. Barbarians`
    - `Percentage of Optimal Cities`
    - `Corruption`
    - `Max Gov't Transition Time`
    - `Cost Factor`
    - `AI to AI Trade`
- `AI Bonuses`
  - Starting AI combat bonuses:
    - `Offensive Land Units`
    - `Defensive Land Units`
- `Additional Starting Units`
  - Extra start amounts for predefined slots
- `Unit Support`
  - `Base Bonus`
  - `Per Settlement`

## Notes
- Quint groups DIFF primarily by gameplay theme, not by record layout.
